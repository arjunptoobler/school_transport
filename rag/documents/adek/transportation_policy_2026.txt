ADEK Student Transportation Policy — Key Regulations (2026)

Section 14.2 — Guardian Handover
Students under Grade 3 (Age 9) must never be left unattended at a drop-off point.
If an authorized guardian is not present, the driver or school bus supervisor must
retain the student on board, notify school dispatch, and return the student safely
to the school campus or local security precinct.

Section 14.3 — Supervisor Duties
Every school bus carrying students below Grade 4 must have a designated bus supervisor
(female for female students). The supervisor is responsible for:
- Verifying student headcount before departure and at every stop.
- Ensuring all students are buckled before the vehicle moves.
- Logging any behavioral incidents or medical events in the ADEK digital portal within 30 minutes.

Section 4 — Incident Reporting
Any safety hazard, student behavior stage escalation, or missing supervisor incident
must be logged in the electronic system within 30 minutes of occurrence.
Parent notifications must be sent immediately via registered contact channels.
Operators must submit a formal report to ADEK within 24 hours for any High-severity event.

Section 7 — Driver Permit Requirements
All drivers must hold a valid UAE school bus permit issued by PASS (Permit and Authorization
Support System). Permits expire annually and require:
- Medical fitness certificate (valid 12 months)
- Criminal background clearance
- ADEK-approved safe driving training completion
- Defensive driving refresher every 3 years
