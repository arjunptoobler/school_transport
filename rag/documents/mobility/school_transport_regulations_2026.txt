Abu Dhabi Mobility — School Transport Regulations (2026)

Section 2.1 — Driver Mobile Phone and Distraction Policy
Operating a school transport vehicle while holding or viewing a mobile phone or any
electronic device is strictly prohibited at all times.
Penalty: AED 5,000 fine, 24 black points, immediate suspension of school bus transport permit.
Repeat offence within 12 months results in permanent permit revocation.

Section 3.4 — Speed Limits in School Zones
Maximum speed within 500m of any registered school during school hours: 40 km/h.
Speed limit on all school bus routes between 06:30–08:30 and 13:00–16:00: 60 km/h.
Penalty: AED 3,000 fine, 12 black points, compulsory safe-driving course.

Section 5 — Vehicle Inspection Requirements
All school transport vehicles must pass:
- Daily pre-trip checklist (driver + supervisor sign-off required)
- Weekly full inspection logged in the digital fleet management system
- Annual roadworthiness certificate from Abu Dhabi Ports and Transport Authority

Inspection failure items that result in immediate grounding:
- Brake pressure below threshold
- Non-functional emergency exit release
- Inoperative safety cameras (cabin or external)
- HVAC failure during months April–October
- GPS tracker offline for more than 15 minutes

Section 6 — Route Deviation and GPS Compliance
All school buses must follow pre-approved routes loaded in the fleet management system.
Any deviation exceeding 200m triggers an automatic alert to the operator control centre.
Planned route changes require 48-hour advance approval from Abu Dhabi Mobility.
